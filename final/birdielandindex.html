Come Explore Birdie Land

For my analysis and comparison of two library policies I chose two Canadian university libraries: Western and Dalhousie University. I chose these libraries because their policies were clearly defined and labelled, unlike a few other university libraries in which I had to search a little too hard for a document that is suppose to be easily accessible for all. In searching the Western University library site I found the circulation policy within a long list titled: ‘Access Codes for Western’ and the customer service policy was located after accessing the Home page and then the ‘Loan Policies and Fines’ section. For Dalhousie University I found the circulation policy under a tab called ‘Borrow’ and the customer service policy was a separate pdf located under a title of ‘Policy Documents.’ The average library user would have no trouble finding either of these documents.
